Who am I?

Hello, I am Klyd D. Manuel from BSIT-NW3A, and I currently live in Pag-Asa, Dinalupihan, Bataan.
I am 20 years old, and I will be turning 21 this upcoming May 8.
I am a student at Bataan Peninsula State University - Main, currently in my third year.
It feels great to keep moving forward despite the challenges and exhaustion that come with being a student.
Even though it can be tiring, I still find joy in learning and growing.

I have many hobbies that keep me entertained and motivated. I enjoy playing games, watching movies and anime, and reading books and mangas.
I also love food and trying different kinds of dishes whenever I get the chance.
Another thing I enjoy is drinking coffee, which has become a big part of my daily routine.
There have been times when I drank coffee four times a day just to satisfy my cravings.

Currently, I spend my free time playing games like Valorant and League of Legends.
These games bring back memories of my childhood when I used to enjoy gaming without any worries.
However, I now have more responsibilities and must focus on my academic tasks.
My priority is to complete my schoolwork and help my groupmates so we can all pass this semester.
While gaming is fun, I know that my studies are more important.

Aside from gaming, I also have a passion for coding and website design.
I enjoy figuring out how things work and coming up with creative solutions to problems.
When designing a website, I prefer to create a prototype first before fully developing it.
I constantly explore new website designs and concepts to improve my skills.
Although I am not yet an expert, I find joy in learning and expanding my knowledge in this field.

Despite the challenges, I remain determined to keep pushing forward.
Being a student is not easy, but I believe that perseverance and dedication will help me succeed.
I continue to balance my academics, hobbies, and self-improvement to make the most out of my college years.
Every experience, whether good or bad, teaches me something valuable.
No matter how tough things get, I will keep striving for success and personal growth.
